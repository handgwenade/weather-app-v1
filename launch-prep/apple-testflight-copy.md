# Apple TestFlight Copy

## Beta App Description

RoadSignal is a weather and road-awareness app designed to give a fast operational view of current conditions, short-term outlook, official alerts, and road-related context for saved locations.

## What To Test

Please test location switching, adding, editing, and deleting saved locations, persistence after app restart, and the overall behavior of the Home, Road, Conditions, Alerts, and Locations screens.

Watch for:

- broken navigation
- confusing empty states
- stale timestamps
- inaccurate location behavior
- any screen where unavailable data is not explained clearly
