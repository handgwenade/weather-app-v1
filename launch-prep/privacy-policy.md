# Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy for RoadSignal

Effective date: [INSERT DATE]

RoadSignal provides weather, alert, and road-related information for user-selected locations.

## Information We Collect

RoadSignal may process the following information to operate the app:

- **Location data:** If you allow location access, the app may use your device location to help show nearby weather and road-related information.
- **Saved locations:** Locations you add, edit, or save in the app may be stored so they remain available the next time you use RoadSignal.

RoadSignal does not sell your personal information.

## How We Use Information

We use information only to operate app features, including to:

- display weather, alert, and road-related information for selected locations
- support saved-location features
- help determine relevant nearby conditions when location access is enabled

## Sharing and Third Parties

To provide app functionality, RoadSignal may send limited request data to third-party service providers such as weather, map, alert, hosting, or road-data providers. This is used only to retrieve the information needed for app features.

## Location Permissions

If you grant permission, RoadSignal may use your device location to help determine relevant nearby conditions. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

## Data Retention

Saved locations and similar app data may remain stored until you remove them or uninstall the app.

## Your Choices

You can:

- disable location permissions in device settings
- remove saved locations within the app
- stop using the app at any time

## Children’s Privacy

RoadSignal is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
