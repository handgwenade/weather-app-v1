## Full Description

RoadSignal helps you keep an eye on weather, official alerts, and road-related conditions for the places you care about most.

Use RoadSignal to:

- view current weather conditions
- check the next several hours at a glance
- review official alerts affecting a location
- monitor road-related context and travel concerns where available
- save, edit, and manage important locations

RoadSignal is built for quick operational awareness, with a simple layout that helps you move between weather, conditions, alerts, and locations without digging through clutter.

Features include:

- saved locations
- short-term forecast visibility
- official alert awareness
- road-focused context where supported
- clean tab-based navigation for fast checking

Road and weather conditions can change quickly. Always use local observation and official sources when making travel or safety decisions.
